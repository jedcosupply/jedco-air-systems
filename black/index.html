Air-Side Rep Agency

The Lines You Spec.
The Coverage You Expect.

JEDCO Air Systems is an air-side manufacturer’s rep serving Metro Atlanta and the Southeast. We carry the commercial lines — terminal units, fans, dampers, diffusers, louvers, IAQ — and handle the full rep workflow: spec support, submittals, and order management from award through delivery.

01

Spec Support

We work with contractors and engineers during design to get the right product written in — sized correctly, priced accurately, and approvable by the EOR. We’re reachable when the questions come up.

02

Submittals

Once the job is awarded, we prepare and track submittals. We know what’s due, who needs it, and when — and we don’t make you follow up to find out where things stand.

03

Order Management

After approval, we coordinate with the manufacturer and track delivery to the job site. Stock items ship same-day from JEDCO Supply’s warehouse in Suwanee when lead time isn’t an option.
